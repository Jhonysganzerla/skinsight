# Skinsight Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-26

Skinsight does not collect, store or transmit personal data.

The extension reads publicly available data from the following websites when
you visit them:

- skinsmonkey.com
- csfloat.com
- pirateswap.com
- cs.money
- steamcommunity.com (only when you explicitly request a Steam price lookup)
- api.skinport.com (price oracle, cached for 5 minutes per Skinport's terms)

This data is processed locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any
external server controlled by us.

Your preferences (which mode is enabled, currency conversion rate, etc.) are
saved using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API, which keeps them on your
device only.

The extension contains a bundled database of rare CS2 stickers
(`rare_stickers.json`), updated via extension releases.

If you have questions, open an issue at
`https://github.com/jhonysganzerla/skinsight`.
